# Subskill: Document Conversion

## Use this when
Converting PDFs, Word documents, packets, brochures, instructions, or long documents into digital-first web pages, forms, or structured content.

## Goals

- Preserve required substance while improving usability.
- Break document-centered content into task-centered content.
- Remove duplication, outdated material, and print-only assumptions.
- Identify content that should remain downloadable.

## Conversion workflow

1. Inventory the document
   - Title
   - Owner
   - Audience
   - Purpose
   - Current format
   - Last updated date, if known
   - Required/legal sections
   - Transactional steps
   - Attachments or forms

2. Classify each section
   - Keep as web content
   - Convert to an online form
   - Keep as downloadable document
   - Merge with another page
   - Archive or retire
   - Send for legal, policy, records, accessibility, or program review

3. Restructure around the task
   Common web structure:

   - What this is
   - Who can use it
   - Before you start
   - What you need
   - How to apply, pay, report, request, register, or appeal
   - What happens next
   - Fees and deadlines
   - Get help
   - Related forms or documents

4. Rewrite headings and labels
   Use resident action language instead of document section labels.

5. Preserve official content carefully
   Do not alter legal, regulatory, rights, appeal, penalty, or notice language without review.

## Conversion patterns

### Packet to task page
Turn a packet into:

- Landing page
- Eligibility section
- Step-by-step instructions
- Online form or downloadable form
- Required documents checklist
- Confirmation and next steps

### Brochure to service page
Turn a brochure into:

- Service summary
- Eligibility or audience guidance
- How to get the service
- Contact information
- Related programs

### Long PDF to structured page series
Turn a long document into:

- Overview page
- Subpages by task or topic
- Downloadable official document only if needed
- Cross-links between related steps

## Review checklist

- The converted content is organized by resident task.
- Required official language is preserved or flagged.
- Duplicate content is removed or consolidated.
- Downloadable documents are justified.
- The new structure includes prerequisites, steps, deadlines, and next steps.
