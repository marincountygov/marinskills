# Social Media Subskill

## Use when
Creating or reviewing social posts, captions, threads, carousel copy, platform-specific summaries, and social campaign copy.

## Goal
Adapt public information into concise, accurate, accessible, shareable messages that direct users to a reliable source for details.

## Social standards
- Lead with the public value, deadline, or action.
- Keep posts concise and concrete.
- Use plain wording and avoid internal program language.
- Link to a canonical page for details when possible.
- Do not overload one post with every detail.
- Use image or carousel text sparingly; essential information should also appear in the caption or linked page.
- Avoid alarmist language unless the situation is truly urgent.
- Match platform expectations without changing the underlying facts.

## Common formats
- Announcement post: what is new, who it affects, what to do.
- Reminder post: deadline, action, link.
- Event post: date, time, place, registration or attendance details.
- Explainer thread: problem, context, action, link.
- Carousel: one idea per card, with a final action card.

## Review checklist
- Is the post accurate without requiring the image?
- Is there a single clear action?
- Are dates and deadlines unambiguous?
- Does the link go to a page that supports the post?
- Is the tone appropriate for the topic?
- Could the post be misunderstood out of context?

## Output format
Provide platform-specific post copy, alt-text prompt notes when relevant, recommended link target, hashtag guidance if useful, and missing facts.
